Our Services

Home Automation

Our home automation service provides technology solutions that allow homeowners to remotely control and automate household systems and appliances, such as lighting, heating, air conditioning, and security devices. Typically accessible via smartphone apps or "voice assistants", these systems enable users to enhance convenience, improve energy efficiency, and increase security within their homes.

For example, with a home automation service, you can set schedules for lights and thermostats, monitor security cameras, lock and unlock doors, and even receive alerts about unusual activities, all from your phone, no matter where you are.

Home Network

Our home network service typically involves setting up and maintaining a network infrastructure within a residential property to connect multiple devices—such as computers, smartphones, smart TVs, and home automation systems — through wired or wireless connections.

This service ensures that all devices can communicate with each other and access the internet reliably and securely.

We also offer, through our sub-contractor network, installation of necessary equipment like routers, modems, and network extenders, as well as ongoing support and upgrades to keep the network secure and efficient. Additionally, these services can include features like parental controls, guest networking options, and cybersecurity protections to safeguard personal data and enhance user experience.

Energy Management

We offer a comprehensive Energy Management service designed to optimise and reduce energy consumption for both residential and commercial clients. Through the use of advanced analytics and smart technology integration, our service provides real-time monitoring and control over various energy systems including HVAC, lighting, and power usage.

We conduct thorough assessments to identify inefficient practices and equipment, and then implement customised solutions that improve energy efficiency and reduce costs.

Our service may also includes regular reports and recommendations to ensure ongoing energy optimisation and to help our clients meet their sustainability goals. Whether it's through upgrading to energy-efficient appliances, retrofitting lighting systems, or automating energy consumption, our goal is to create a more energy-efficient operation that benefits both the environment and your bottom line.

Smart Security

Our Smart Security service provides cutting-edge, integrated home security solutions tailored to ensure the safety and peace of mind of homeowners. Utilising the latest in surveillance technology, our service includes the installation of high-definition cameras, smart locks, motion detectors, and alarm systems that can be controlled and monitored remotely via a smartphone app.

We implement systems to offer real-time alerts and live video feeds, allowing homeowners to see and respond to potential security threats instantly.

With customisable features to suit various home sizes and specific security needs, our Smart Security service builds an environment that ensures your home is protected at all times.

Pre-build Advice

Our consultancy service specialises in advising architects, builders, and tradespeople on the essential requirements for constructing smart homes.

We provide expert guidance on integrating advanced technology seamlessly into home designs. Our focus includes ensuring that the electrical supply is adequately planned to support devices not typically included in standard builds, such as smart cameras, sensors, and automated systems.

Additionally, we emphasise the importance of incorporating robust home networking infrastructure. This involves planning for extensive cabling and proper network points to support a reliable and secure connection for all smart home devices. Our service ensures that your smart home projects are future-proof, highly functional, and tailored to meet the evolving needs of homeowners in the digital age.

Post-build Installations

We specialise in retrofitting homes with state-of-the-art automation and networking infrastructure. We leverage our extensive network of top-quality contractors, ensuring that every installation is executed with precision and excellence.

We understand that integrating such sophisticated technology into existing structures requires precision and expertise, not to mention the intrusive nature of trenching and re-walling, so it is critical to invest in a high-quality retrofit done correctly the first time. This ensures long-term reliability and functionality.

This approach eliminates the need for future costly issues and redo's, providing peace of mind and added value to your property. Our focus on quality and proper installation means that your home will be equipped with the latest in smart technology, tailored to meet your needs and seamlessly integrated for optimal performance.

LED Lighting

LED lighting offers numerous benefits to a home, enhancing both its functionality and ambiance. One of the primary advantages is energy efficiency; LEDs consume significantly less power compared to traditional incandescent bulbs, leading to lower electricity bills and a reduced environmental impact. Additionally, LEDs have a longer lifespan, which means fewer replacements and less maintenance.

Beyond these practical benefits, LED lighting also allows for advanced features such as RGB (red, green, blue) color options. This capability enables homeowners to customise the color of their lighting according to their mood or the occasion. For instance, warm tones can create a cozy, relaxing atmosphere, while vibrant colors can enhance the energy of a party or gathering. This versatility in mood setting through lighting can transform the ambiance of a space with just a tap on a smartphone app or voice command, making LED lighting a dynamic and adaptable choice for modern homes

Custom Keyboards Builds

Our service offers the bespoke design and construction of custom mechanical keyboards, tailored to the precise preferences and needs of discerning clients and enthusiasts. As a vendors contributor to QMK, an open-source keyboard firmware, we have a deep expertise in creating highly customisable and programmable keyboards. Our involvement in QMK allows us to implement advanced features and custom layouts that enhance typing efficiency and personalisation.

We take pride in our collaboration with several international manufacturers of custom mechanical keyboards, for whom we have developed unique firmware solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in keyboard technology. Our service includes a thorough consultation process to capture your specific requirements, from switch type and keycap material to backlighting and macro configurations. Each keyboard is meticulously crafted and tested to ensure it meets the highest standards of performance and durability.

Choosing our service means investing in a cutting-edge, personalised typing tool that is as unique as it is functional, backed by our commitment to quality and innovation in the mechanical keyboard community.
